Investigating News Bias using NLP

Explored political bias in news reporting using Natural Language Processing techniques and hypothesis testing.

Highlights:

  • Scraped headlines from left, center, and right-leaning sources.
  • Performed sentiment analysis using VADER and TextBlob libraries.
  • Used statistical hypothesis testing to verify bias significance.
  • Visualized polarity and subjectivity differences with Matplotlib and Seaborn.
